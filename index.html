Open Source Observability for
LLMs

An LLM operations tool designed to provide developers with complete capabilities for monitoring, analysing, and optimising LLM applications.

Get Started

Why use Doku?

Granular Usage Insights

Fine-tune LLM performance and costs to achieve maximum efficiency and scalability.

Real-Time Data Streaming

Streams data to let you visualise your data and make quick decisions and modifications.

Zero Added Latency

Ensures that data is processed quickly without affecting the performance of your application.

Visualize and analyze

Doku UI helps you to explore LLM costs, token consumption, performance indicators, and user interactions in a straightforward interface.

Observability Platforms

Connect to popular observability systems with ease, including Datadog and Grafana Cloud, to export data automatically.

Seamlessly export your data to
your favorite observability platforms
